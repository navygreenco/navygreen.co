Who we are

NavyGreen is a design studio rooted in clean lines, warm details, and real-life function.

We believe great design doesn’t have to be complicated. Our approach is simple and elevated: start with what you have, make a few high-leverage changes, and bring the whole space into balance.

Whether you need help with a one-room refresh, sourcing the right pieces, or a creative flip idea, we keep it calm, clear, and tailored to you.
