Who we are

NavyGreen is a design studio rooted in clean lines, warm details, and real-life function.

We believe great design doesn’t have to be complicated. Our approach is minimal, elevated, and practical: start with what you have, make a few high-impact changes, and bring the whole space into balance.

Whether you need a quick refresh, help sourcing pieces, or a creative flip idea, we keep it simple and make it feel like you.
